fried mackerel

crispy fish for crispy friends
  • mackerel
  • garlic
  • salt
  1. gut and salt mackerel before you want to cook - set aside for 20-30 minutes, then rinse off salt.
  2. heat oil in a pan till very hot. if a fillet, place the mackerel skin side down: otherwise just, put it in the pan. DON'T TOUCH IT for 3 minutes (more if it's a big guy). flip over, repeat. check the flesh next to the spine to see if it's cooked.
  3. for a little extra flavour, chuck in some garlic right at the end
