beef and peppers noodles

"lots of oil"
  • ground beef // shredded steak
  • cumin seeds
  • light soy

  • sichuan peppercorns
  • 5 spice
  • green pepper
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • msg if u wanna (let's face it, this is not a health dish)
  • normal wheat/egg noodles, or rice vermicelli
  • lots of veg oil so noodles don’t stick
  1. chop and fry the green pepper until it starts to blacken. add the spices, garlic and ground beef. don't overdo them, the beef should be brown but not chewy
  2. bring water to the boil and cook noodles (the thin white chinese ones are good for this). strain noodles well, rinsing them in cold water through the strainer to get rid of extra starch. immediately add to the pan (so noodles don’t get sticky), and fry over high heat with lots of oil, the pepper and beef mixture. Use a wok on high heat, or a non-stick pan.
  3. the aim is for the noodles/veg to fry and not to steam in there (so don’t overfill the pan). For crisper noodles, don’t mix too much to allow noodles plenty of contact with the heat.
