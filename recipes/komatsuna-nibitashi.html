komatsuna nibitashi

(mustard greens and fried tofu)

the price per gram of reliable mart's aburaage feels totally outrageous (like spending $5 on air), but it goes a surprisingly long way
  • mustard greens
  • aburaage

  • bonito dashi

  • tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp mirin

  • 1 tbsp light soy

  • 1 tbsp sake
  1. heat sugar, soy, sake, mirin, dashi and a small amount of water in a pan.
  2. remove the stalks from the mustard greens, chop and simmer in the mixture until starting to go soft. add in sliced aburaage, and cook for another minute.
  3. serve with white rice.
