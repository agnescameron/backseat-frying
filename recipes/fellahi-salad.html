fellahi salad

the name means 'peasant salad', and we ate a lot of it on the hill at sakiya. other 'fellahi' cuisines include tea made with sage and 5 cups of sugar, and emptying half a bottle of olive oil over lunch.
  • nice ripe tomatoes
  • red onion
  • long green hot pepper (like a hungarian pepper)
  • cucumber
  • garlic
  • mint
  • parsley
  • lemon
  1. dice the onions, peppers, cucumber and tomato into tiny chunks. finely chop the garlic, parsley and mint, and mix them all together. drizzle with lemon, and olive oil. salt well.
good with flatbread. if you can't find hottish smallish green peppers, probably don't substitute with a big green pepper as they're a bit watery for this: instead grind some black pepper onto it to replace the heat.
