tasty miso

tastier than thou
  1. very very simple you get your miso (i like shiro miso, the white stuff, cause it's creamy but you can also use red miso), bonito dashi, heat in a pan with some wakame seaweed
  2. good additions: cubes of tofu, sesame oil on top, chopped spring onions
this makes a great breakfast with white rice, tinned fish, furikake and umeboshi
