soba soup

clear, refreshing and a little bit fishy
  • soba noodles

  • sake
  • mirin

  • light soy

  • tsp bonito dashi

  • (optional) pinch wakame seaweed

  1. heat water in a pan and add in a glug each of mirin, sake and soy, along with a teaspoon of dashi powder and the wakame (wakame really expands, so start with a pinch and add more if you like).
  2. in a different pan, bring water to the boil and cook the noodles in lots of water, for around 4 minutes. drain, cover in the soup base, and add toppings.
  3. if you're looking to impress anyone (even just yourself), as you're putting the noodles into the bowl, use chopsticks to pick them up in a straight sheet, and fold them at the bottom before pouring on the stock. it's simple but makes the dish surprisingly sexy.
