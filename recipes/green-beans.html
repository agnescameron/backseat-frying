green beans with garlic and sichuan peppercorns

a hot salad
  • green beans
  • garlic, finely chopped
  • salt
  • sichuan peppercorns

  • soy

  1. Toast peppercorns first in hot dry pan. Remove them.
  2. fry green beans with a little oil, salt and peppercorns till they go soft and start to blister and blacken on the outside. You may want to put a lid on the pan for this bit as it can get smoky and oil can sizzle a lot.
  3. add in splash of soy, garlic (and a little bit of water), fry fast until the liquid disappears then serve.
