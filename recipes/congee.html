  1. put leftover cooked rice in rice cooker (or if too much, in a big pan), with 4x as much water as there is rice. you get nice congee after 2 runs through the rice cooker, or about an hour simmering on the stove. May need to be topped up with water regularly, and stir to prevent lumps/sticking to bottom.
