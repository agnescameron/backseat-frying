tom yum

tasty paste, sour and spicy on a cold day
  • a couple of chopped tomatoes
  • tom yum paste

  • shrimp paste

    possible contents:
  • shrimp
  • rice vermicelli
  • chinese/thai aubergines
  • chopped bottle gourd/chayote
  • fried tofu
  • small mushrooms
  • coriander leaves
  1. if you wanna use a slow-cooking vegetable like a chinese obe or a gourd, or some shallots, chop into slices and fry on a high heat till browning/softening
  2. fry tom yum paste and shrimp paste in the pan for a few seconds, then add some water, and bring it gently to the boil
  3. add in the chopped tomatoes, and simmer until soft
  4. add mushrooms, and things that want to simmer for a few minutes
  5. add in shrimp, fried tofu anything else that cooks fast
serve topped with chopped coriander leaves. add a splash of coconut milk if you'd like it creamy. some day we will make this soup from scratch and it will be really good. if you want an even less from scratch version, MAMA Shrimp Creamy Tom Yum instant noodles are really really good (best instant noodle), especially with a fried egg.
