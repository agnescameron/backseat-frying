tom yum

tasty paste, sour and spicy on a cold day
  • a couple of chopped tomatoes
  • tom yum paste

  • shrimp
  • fried tofu
  • small mushrooms
  • coriander
  1. mix a tablespoon of tom yum paste with some water, and bring it gently to the boil
  2. add in the chopped tomatoes, and simmer until soft
  3. add mushrooms, and things that take a little bit of time to cook
  4. add in shrimp, fried tofu anything else that cooks fast
serve topped with chopped coriander. some day we will make this soup from scratch and it will be really good. if you want an even less from scratch version, MAMA Shrimp Creamy Tom Yum instant noodles are really really good (best instant noodle).
