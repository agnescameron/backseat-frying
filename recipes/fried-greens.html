fried greens

"do it up" (encouragement from zach). these go well with everything.
  • greens
  • garlic
  • soy
    /salt

  1. chop a garlic clove or 2, and wash the greens
  2. heat oil in the wok until it shimmers, then put the garlic in and fry till it starts to sizzle (you want the oil to get the flavour but you don’t want it to go brown)
  3. put in the greens, add soy or salt and a little bit of water, put a lid on the pan to steam soft if they’re big. important that the pan's not too full, and everything cooks really hot.
