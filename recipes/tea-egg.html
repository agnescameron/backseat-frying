tea egg

egg party!
a truly iconic snack, put it in your pocket and discover it later, joyous. have it on a soup! with a stew! on noodles! good hot or cold, makes a great gift (popular with the ladies).
  • many eggs
  • 2 tbsp light soy

  • 2 tbsp dark soy

  • whole black peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp loose black tea
  • 5 star anise

  • tsp five spice powder

  • 1/2 tsp MSG (optional but ultimately delicious)

  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  1. put enough water in a pan to submerge the eggs completely, and add in all of the other ingredients. bring to the boil, then leave to cool for half an hour
  2. take the eggs out, and crack them all over with a spoon. return to the pan and leave them to sit for some hours, or overnight in the fridge.
