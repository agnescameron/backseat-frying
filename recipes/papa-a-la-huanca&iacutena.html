papa a la huancaína

come to papa
  • 1 tbsp aji amarillo paste

  • chunk of queso fresco
  • 2 tbsp cream or evaporated milk
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 crushed crackers
  • 3 yellow potatos
  1. slice the potatoes into thickish rounds, and put in a pan of salted water. bring to the boil
  2. put all the other ingredients into the blender, blend till smooth. add salt to taste.
  3. when the potatoes are soft, cover with the sauce
this sauce also goes great with things like boiled eggs and white corn. have eaten this dish with an omelette made with fried peppers and leftover queso fresco (and with zach!), was really nice.
