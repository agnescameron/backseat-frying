cabbage thoran

curried salad
  • some shallots, or an onion, diced
  • half a cabbage, shredded
  • cumin seeds
  • mustard seeds
  • chopped green chillies
  • curry leaves
  • turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • tbsp grated coconut
  • coriander
  1. heat oil in a pan, and fry the mustard seeds till they start to pop. Add in the cumin, then the diced shallots.
  2. fry the shallots till tender and starting to brown, then add the chillies, turmeric, hing and curry leaves.
  3. add in the shredded cabbage with some salt and saute, making sure to mix well. put a tight-fitting lid on the pan and cook on a low heat (don't add any water! It will steam by itself!)
  4. after 10 minutes, take of the lid and stir. Add in the grated coconut, mix well, then top with the coriander leaves
This is crunchy and fresh, and goes well with potato masala and dosa. Thorans are dry dishes, so no adding moisture to this!
