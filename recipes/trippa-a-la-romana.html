trippa a la romana

tripe in the roman style

"it's my third stomach of the day"
  • 2lb tripe
  • 4 strips bacon
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1 small tin tomatoes
  • white wine
  • parmesan
  • olive oil
  • mint (optional)
  1. boil the tripe hard for 15min, then drain and wash, to get rid of the tripey aroma (don't worry, there will be plenty aroma left to go round). Once it's cool, slice into fine strips.
  2. saute the chopped bacon, then add in diced onion, garlic, carrot and celery. sweat over a low heat until soft. add in the tripe and fry for another few minutes.
  3. Add a splash of white wine and, once evaporated, the tonatoes. Simmer until the tripe is tender (about 30min or so). Add in some crushed red chillies here if you like, too.
  4. About 5min before serving, add in a handful of chopped mint. Serve hot, topped with grated parmesan.
This dish combines the comfort of pasta and tomato sauce, with the thrill of tripe. It goes great with bread and lightly cooked greens.
