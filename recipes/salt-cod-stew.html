salt cod stew

zuppa di baccala

oscar liked it and sultan's mum called us smart hippies, an experience we'd all choose to replicate
  • salt cod (u gotta start soaking it 2 days before, change water every so often)

  • olive oil
  • lots of garlic
  • onion
  • 2 fennels
  • cup of white wine
  • chopped parsley
  • chopped basil
  • 4 potatoes
  • 2 cans tinned tomatoes
  • parsley
  • bay leaf
  • chilli flakes
  1. gently fry garlic + salt in lots of olive oil (low heat), then add sliced onion and fennel bulb + cook slowly till soft.
  2. turn up the heat and add white wine, soften more than add potatoes. add about 1/3 of the fennel fronds (chopped finely) + tomatoes, chilli flakes, basil and parsley. add water and simmer till potatoes cooked.
  3. chop salt cod into 2 inch chunks, and simmer (don't boil!), till everything's soft and nice.
  4. probably needs more salt at this point (but wait till the salt cod's gone in till you add more).
This delicious recipe comes to us from Linda's Italian Table, a truly wild food blog.
