potato masala

smooth yet punchy potato
  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • mustard seeds
  • green chilli
  • 1 tbsp chana dal
  • turmeric
  • asafoetida
  • ginger
  • 7 or 8 curry leaves (these are hard to find but worth it)
  1. chop the potatoes, and put in a pan of salted water to boil
  2. while the potatoes are cooking, heat oil in a pan, and fry the chana dal until they turn golden brown
  3. chop the onion into slices (don't mince it!) and fry with the chana dal on a low heat till translucent
  4. add curry leaves, chopped green chilli, ginger and mustard seeds, and fry together
  5. add in the turmeric and asafoetida, then add some water and simmer for a few minutes
  6. once the potatoes are soft, mash them, and stir into the onions. season with salt, and a pinch of sugar, and simmer until thick
goes well with dosa and pickles
