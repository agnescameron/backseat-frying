cabbage omelette

the thinking man's kimchi pancake
  • 3 or 4 eggs
  • light soy

  • chinese cabbage
  • garlic
  1. chop cabbage into very fine strips. chop garlic and fry till you smell it, then add the cabbage and soy. beat eggs, and pour over when the cabbage is soft. flip as the top starts to solidify. serve w gochujang and kewpie
there's a bit of an art to frying the chinese cabbage - you want the cabbage to be soft but you don't want it to overcook: it'll release lots of water into the pan, start to steam instead of fry, and then the eggs won't be so nice. so - as soon as it's soft, put the eggs in, and make sure the pan's hot and not too full (to make sure the cabbage fries instead of steams).
